Hi! I set up a screen recorder for you. It's just a web page — nothing to
install, and your videos stay on your computer.

Save this link (or keep this message so you can find it):

    https://coffee-discord.github.io/easy-screen-recorder/

HOW TO RECORD:

1. Click the link. (If it doesn't open in Chrome or Edge, tell me and
   I'll help — it needs one of those two.)

2. Click the big red "Start recording" button.

3. A popup appears. Click the PICTURE OF YOUR SCREEN, tick the little
   box near the bottom that mentions SHARING AUDIO, then click the blue
   "Share" button.

4. It counts down 3… 2… 1… and then it's recording everything on your
   screen. You can minimize the recorder window.

5. When you're done, click the "Stop sharing" button floating at the
   bottom of your screen. That's it — the video saves itself into your
   DOWNLOADS folder.

WANT YOUR VOICE IN THE VIDEO?
Turn the "Microphone" switch ON before you press record. The first time,
the browser asks for permission — click "Allow". Talk normally; the green
bar moves when it hears you.

WANT IT AS AN ICON ON YOUR DESKTOP?
On the recorder page there's a button that says "Put this on my desktop
as an app". Click it, then click Install. After that it opens like any
other program.

If anything looks confusing, the page itself will usually tell you what
to do — and you can always call me!
